HOW IT WORKS

Zonpace is here to help you search the vacant land or empty space for rent (to all of your purposes). Whether you’re a space owner or a searcher looking for somewhere to rent, here’s how Zonepace works in 4 simple steps:

For Searchers

Search

  • Enter the location where you need renting.
  • You can enter a city, street name or postcode into the search box to bring up listings that are most relevant to you.
  • Filters allow you to narrow down your search, depending on your requirements.

Choose

  • A browser will appear the listings of which includes information related to your search keywords in the search box frame.
  • Choose the one which best suits, you can view more details about price, area, map, policy, service…on where you chose.

Book

  • To be able to start booking, simply ﬁll in the information in table form.
  • If you are afraid your information leaked to the outside, Zonepace can ensure your privacy.
  • Your information is only passed to the owner to conﬁrmed tenants.

Active

  • Once you’ve received conﬁrmation of your booking from Zonpace, your reservations completed.

For Owners

Create your listing & Plot a space

  • On the form is available, you just need to enter the details and description of your rental space to create your free listing. As detailed as possible, this will make guests appreciate the place for rent.

Update photos & calendar

  • To enhance your listing, you can add images to your space (up to 10 photos).
  • Also Zonpace provides availability calendar, which you can customize the time that you want to hire

Wait for bookings

  • If you're renting out your space on a daily basis just wait for bookings to come in.
  • Conﬁrmation will be sent to you via text messages via the phone number you provided us.

Manage service

  • We offer a fully managed service so renting out a space is as hassle-free as it can possibly be. Monthly payments will be automatically made to you, we’ll ensure there are no missed payments, and our dedicated team will deal with any queries.
